Iowa Manure Spills 2010-2019

These visualizations are based on data from the Iowa Department of Natural Resources' Hazardous Spill Incident Database. Iowa law requires those people who store, handle or transport manure and other animal waste products to report spills within six hours of onset or discovery. Nutrients and pathogens held within manure can make their way into water sources after spills, causing human illnesses and ecological disturbances, including toxic algae blooms and fish kills.

Data ends at April 6 2019.
