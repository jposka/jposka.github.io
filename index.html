Resume

My greatest strength as a writer is making seemingly dry, dense information engaging and clear. Whether I am writing about city affairs, scientific findings or agricultural policy, people tell me I have a knack for making them care. Three years in Iowa as a journalist and environmental scholar have steered my interests especially toward agri-environmental reporting, though I enjoy writing about almost anything. I am learning to tell stories through various multimedia as well and have developed a particular interest in the journalistic potential of data analysis and Geographic Information Systems.

I believe the pieces linked below represent my strongest work, mostly for the Daily Iowan, Dubuque Telegraph Herald and Civil Eats. I will graduate from the University of Iowa with degrees in journalism and geography in May 2020 and look forward to starting my career. While I would be thrilled to find a position related to environmental, agricultural or data reporting, I am open to almost anything that will let me tell meaningful human stories with integrity.
